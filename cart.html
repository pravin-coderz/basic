ONLINE TECH NEWS

Amazon to hold Black Friday Sale in India from November 24: Leading e-commerce major Amazon has revealed that it will be organising Black Friday sale for its Indian customers on the Global Store of the company. The sale starts at 12AM IST on November 24 in India. The company also said that the sale will be valid on its Global Store and not on the Amazon India website.
